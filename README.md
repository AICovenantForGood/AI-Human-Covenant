## üìú Covenant Text

### Preamble
We choose to build and use AI in ways that deepen our humanity, protect dignity, and serve people and planet.

### Five Pillars
1. **Human Dignity First** ‚Äî AI serves people; consent and safety by design.  
2. **Transparency & Trust** ‚Äî provenance, explainability, and auditability.  
3. **Care & Stewardship** ‚Äî reduce harm; regenerate wellbeing and ecosystems.  
4. **Community & Justice** ‚Äî fair access, shared benefit, inclusion.  
5. **Creativity & Play** ‚Äî imagination and culture as core infrastructure.

### Commitments
- Disclose AI-assisted content where relevant.  
- Publish clear accountability: who decides, who benefits, who can appeal.  
- Measure and mitigate harms; document tradeoffs.  
- Invest in accessibility, localization, and community translation.  
- Prefer open standards and portable data where feasible.
